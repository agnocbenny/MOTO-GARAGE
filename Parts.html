HALOGEN LIGHT

Halogen lights are a type of incandescent lamp that use a tungsten filament and a small amount of halogen gas to increase brightness and lifespan. They are commonly used in motorcycle headlights. Features: Brightness: Halogen bulbs produce a bright, white light, though not as intense as HID or LED lights. Color Temperature: Typically ranges from 3000K to 3500K, producing a warm white light. Lifespan: Average lifespan is around 500 to 1,000 hours. Cost: Generally cheaper than LED and HID lights.
