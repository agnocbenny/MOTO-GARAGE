Your Automotive Garage - Seasonal Car Inspection

At Your Motorcycle Garage, we understand that many of our customers are car enthusiasts as well. That's why we offer comprehensive seasonal inspection services for cars to ensure your four-wheeled vehicles are ready for the road ahead.

Our Seasonal Inspection Process

  • Visual Inspection: We perform a visual inspection of your car's exterior and interior to identify any visible issues.
  • Fluid Levels: We check and top up essential fluids, including engine oil, coolant, brake fluid, and windshield washer fluid.
  • Tire Inspection: Our team inspects tire tread depth, tire pressure, and overall tire condition.
  • Brake System Check: We examine the brakes, including pads, rotors, and brake fluid, to ensure safe and efficient braking.
  • Lights and Electrical: We inspect all lights, indicators, and electrical systems for proper function.
  • Undercarriage Examination: We inspect the undercarriage for signs of damage or wear.

Why Choose Us for Seasonal Car Inspections?

  • Skilled Technicians: Our team includes skilled technicians with expertise in both cars and motorcycles.
  • Thoroughness: We leave no detail unchecked to ensure your car is safe and ready for the upcoming season.
  • Transparent Reporting: We provide comprehensive reports detailing the condition of your car and any recommended maintenance or repairs.
  • Convenience: With our combined motorcycle and car services, you can have all your vehicles inspected in one place.

Don't leave the safety and performance of your car to chance. Schedule a seasonal inspection with us today to ensure your car is in top shape for the changing seasons.
